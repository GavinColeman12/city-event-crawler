# Horizon Technologies — Benefits Guide 2026

## Eligibility
Full-time employees (30+ hours/week) are eligible for benefits on the first day of the month following 30 days of employment. Part-time employees are eligible for the 401(k) plan only.

## Health Insurance

### Medical
Horizon offers three medical plan options through Blue Cross Blue Shield of Texas:

**Bronze Plan (HDHP)**
- Monthly premium: $0 employee-only / $280 employee+spouse / $450 employee+family
- Deductible: $3,000 individual / $6,000 family
- Out-of-pocket max: $6,500 individual / $13,000 family
- HSA eligible: Yes — Horizon contributes $750/year to your HSA
- Coinsurance: 80/20 after deductible

**Silver Plan (PPO)**
- Monthly premium: $85 employee-only / $340 employee+spouse / $520 employee+family
- Deductible: $1,500 individual / $3,000 family
- Out-of-pocket max: $4,500 individual / $9,000 family
- HSA eligible: No
- Coinsurance: 80/20 after deductible
- Copays: $25 PCP / $50 specialist / $15 generic Rx

**Gold Plan (PPO)**
- Monthly premium: $165 employee-only / $480 employee+spouse / $720 employee+family
- Deductible: $500 individual / $1,000 family
- Out-of-pocket max: $3,000 individual / $6,000 family
- HSA eligible: No
- Coinsurance: 90/10 after deductible
- Copays: $20 PCP / $40 specialist / $10 generic Rx

### Dental
Horizon offers dental coverage through Delta Dental:
- Monthly premium: $0 employee-only / $35 employee+spouse / $65 employee+family
- Preventive care (cleanings, exams): 100% covered, no deductible
- Basic procedures (fillings, extractions): 80% after $50 deductible
- Major procedures (crowns, bridges): 50% after $50 deductible
- Orthodontia (adult and child): 50% up to $1,500 lifetime max
- Annual maximum: $2,000 per person

### Vision
Vision coverage through VSP:
- Monthly premium: $0 employee-only / $12 employee+spouse / $20 employee+family
- Eye exam: $10 copay, covered annually
- Frames: $150 allowance every 24 months
- Lenses: Covered in full every 12 months
- Contact lens allowance: $150 annually (in lieu of frames)

## Life and Disability Insurance

### Basic Life Insurance
- Horizon provides $50,000 basic life insurance and AD&D at no cost to all full-time employees
- Supplemental life insurance available up to 5x annual salary (evidence of insurability may be required for amounts over 3x)

### Short-Term Disability
- 60% of base salary for up to 12 weeks
- 14-day waiting period
- Fully paid by Horizon

### Long-Term Disability
- 60% of base salary up to $10,000/month
- Begins after short-term disability exhausted (12 weeks)
- Fully paid by Horizon

## 401(k) Retirement Plan

- Eligible after 30 days of employment (full-time and part-time)
- Horizon matches 100% of the first 4% of salary contributed
- Immediate vesting on employee contributions
- Employer match vests over 3 years: 33% at year 1, 66% at year 2, 100% at year 3
- Plan administered by Fidelity
- Traditional and Roth 401(k) options available
- 2026 contribution limit: $23,500 ($31,000 if age 50+)

## Additional Benefits

### Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
Free, confidential counseling and support services for all employees and household members:
- Up to 8 counseling sessions per issue per year
- Available 24/7 by phone: 1-800-555-HELP
- Services include: mental health, substance abuse, financial counseling, legal consultation, work-life balance
- All contacts are 100% confidential and not reported to Horizon

### Professional Development
- $2,500 annual learning stipend per employee for courses, conferences, certifications, and books
- Must be relevant to current role or career development at Horizon
- Pre-approval required from manager
- Submit receipts to HR for reimbursement within 30 days

### Commuter Benefits
- Pre-tax commuter benefits up to $315/month for transit and $315/month for parking
- Administered through WageWorks

### Gym Membership
- $50/month wellness stipend applied as reimbursement for gym membership, fitness classes, or wellness apps
- Submit receipts monthly to HR

### Cell Phone Stipend
- $75/month cell phone stipend for employees required to be reachable outside business hours
- Eligibility determined by manager and HR

## Open Enrollment
Open enrollment for the following year occurs annually in November. Changes to benefits elections can only be made during open enrollment or within 30 days of a qualifying life event (marriage, birth/adoption, loss of other coverage, etc.).
