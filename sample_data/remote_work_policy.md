# Horizon Technologies — Remote & Hybrid Work Policy

## Overview
Horizon operates on a hybrid model. Most roles are eligible for remote work 2–3 days per week, with in-office days required for collaboration, team meetings, and culture building.

## Work Models

### Hybrid (Default)
- In-office Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- Remote Monday and Friday
- Applies to most roles unless your team has different requirements
- Manager may adjust in-office days based on team needs

### Fully Remote
- Available for select roles approved by department head and HR
- Must reside in a state where Horizon has tax registration (currently: TX, CO, CA, NY, FL, WA)
- Required to visit Austin HQ quarterly for team offsites (travel covered by company)
- Must maintain a dedicated workspace with reliable internet (minimum 50 Mbps)

### Fully In-Office
- Required for certain roles (facilities, lab technicians, reception)
- Identified during hiring process

## Remote Work Requirements

### Workspace
- Dedicated, private workspace (not a coffee shop as your primary workspace)
- Reliable internet: minimum 50 Mbps download, 10 Mbps upload
- Ergonomic setup recommended — Horizon provides a $500 one-time home office stipend for new remote/hybrid employees

### Availability
- Must be available during core hours (10:00 AM – 3:00 PM CT) regardless of location
- Camera on for video meetings unless bandwidth issues prevent it
- Respond to Slack messages within 30 minutes during working hours
- Update Slack status when away from desk for 30+ minutes

### Equipment
- Horizon provides: laptop, monitor, keyboard, mouse, headset
- Employees are responsible for: internet connection, desk, chair
- Home office stipend: $500 one-time (request through HR within first 30 days)
- Equipment must be returned upon separation

## International Remote Work
- Employees may work from outside the US for up to 4 weeks per calendar year with manager approval
- Must notify HR at least 2 weeks in advance
- Must maintain overlap with core hours (10 AM – 3 PM CT) — this may limit viable time zones
- Employee is responsible for any tax implications of working abroad
- VPN required for all international remote work

## Requesting a Change in Work Model
To request a change from hybrid to fully remote (or vice versa):
1. Discuss with your manager
2. Submit a request through BambooHR
3. HR and department head review and approve/deny within 10 business days
4. Changes take effect at the start of the following month

## Coworking Spaces
Horizon will reimburse up to $200/month for coworking space membership for fully remote employees. Submit receipts to HR monthly.

## Questions
Contact HR at hr@horizontech.com or your manager for remote work questions.
