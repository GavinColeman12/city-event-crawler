# Horizon Technologies — PTO & Leave Policy

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

Horizon uses a combined PTO bank that covers vacation, sick days, and personal days.

### PTO Accrual

| Tenure | Annual PTO | Accrual Rate |
|--------|-----------|--------------|
| 0–1 years | 15 days (120 hours) | 4.62 hrs/pay period |
| 1–3 years | 20 days (160 hours) | 6.15 hrs/pay period |
| 3–5 years | 23 days (184 hours) | 7.08 hrs/pay period |
| 5–10 years | 25 days (200 hours) | 7.69 hrs/pay period |
| 10+ years | 30 days (240 hours) | 9.23 hrs/pay period |

PTO begins accruing on your first day but cannot be used until after the 90-day introductory period. After the introductory period, accrued PTO is available immediately.

### PTO Carryover
- Maximum carryover: 5 days (40 hours) into the next calendar year
- Carryover days must be used by March 31 or they are forfeited
- PTO does not accrue beyond the annual maximum — once you hit the cap, accrual pauses until you use some PTO

### PTO Requests
- Submit PTO requests through BambooHR at least 2 weeks in advance for planned time off
- For unexpected absences (illness, emergency), notify your manager as soon as possible and submit the request in BambooHR within 24 hours of your return
- Manager approval is required for all PTO
- Requests for more than 5 consecutive business days require HR approval in addition to manager approval
- During peak periods (determined by each department), PTO requests may be limited

### PTO Payout at Separation
Accrued, unused PTO is paid out in the final paycheck for employees who have completed the 90-day introductory period.

## Parental Leave

### Birth Parent Leave
- 16 weeks paid leave at 100% of base salary
- May be taken consecutively or intermittently within 12 months of birth
- Runs concurrently with FMLA where applicable

### Non-Birth Parent Leave (including adoption and foster placement)
- 8 weeks paid leave at 100% of base salary
- May be taken consecutively or intermittently within 12 months of birth, adoption, or placement
- Runs concurrently with FMLA where applicable

### Return to Work
Employees returning from parental leave are guaranteed the same or equivalent position. A gradual return schedule (e.g., 3 days/week for the first 2 weeks) is available upon request.

## Bereavement Leave

- Immediate family (spouse, child, parent, sibling): 5 paid days
- Extended family (grandparent, grandchild, in-law, aunt, uncle): 3 paid days
- Close friend or colleague: 1 paid day
- Additional unpaid leave may be granted at manager's discretion
- "Family" is interpreted broadly — if you have a close relationship with someone not listed above, speak with HR

## Jury Duty
Horizon provides full pay for up to 10 days of jury duty per calendar year. Notify your manager immediately upon receiving a jury summons and provide documentation to HR.

## Voting Leave
Employees may take up to 2 hours of paid time to vote on Election Day if polls are not open for 2 consecutive hours outside of working hours.

## Military Leave
Horizon complies with USERRA and provides unpaid leave for military service with full job protection and benefits continuation. Notify HR as far in advance as possible.

## Family and Medical Leave (FMLA)
Eligible employees (12+ months employed, 1,250+ hours worked in past 12 months) may take up to 12 weeks of unpaid, job-protected leave per year for:
- Birth and care of a newborn
- Placement of a child for adoption or foster care
- Care for an immediate family member with a serious health condition
- Medical leave when unable to work due to a serious health condition

FMLA leave may run concurrently with paid parental leave or short-term disability. Contact HR to initiate FMLA. Medical certification may be required.

## Personal Leave of Absence
Employees may request an unpaid personal leave of absence for up to 30 days for reasons not covered by other leave policies. Approval is at the discretion of your manager and HR. Benefits may continue during personal leave; contact HR for details.
