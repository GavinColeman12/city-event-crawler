# Horizon Technologies — Expense Policy

## Overview
Horizon reimburses employees for reasonable business expenses incurred in the course of their duties. All expenses must be pre-approved by your manager (unless under $50) and submitted within 30 days of being incurred.

## Expense Submission
- Submit all expenses through Expensify
- Include itemized receipts for all expenses over $25
- Categorize expenses correctly (Travel, Meals, Software, etc.)
- Manager approval required before reimbursement is processed
- Reimbursement is processed within 10 business days of approval

## Travel

### Air Travel
- Book economy class for domestic flights under 4 hours
- Premium economy or business class permitted for flights over 4 hours with manager pre-approval
- Book at least 14 days in advance when possible (exceptions for last-minute business needs)
- Use the company's preferred booking tool (Navan) for corporate rates

### Hotels
- Maximum nightly rate: $200/night for most cities; $300/night for high-cost cities (NYC, SF, LA, DC, Boston, Seattle, Chicago)
- Book through Navan for corporate rates
- Incidentals (minibar, movies, spa) are not reimbursable

### Ground Transportation
- Ride-share (Uber/Lyft) or taxi reimbursed for business travel
- Rental cars require manager pre-approval
- Mileage reimbursement for personal vehicle: $0.67/mile (2026 IRS rate)
- Parking and tolls reimbursed with receipts

### Per Diem for Meals During Travel
- Breakfast: $15
- Lunch: $25
- Dinner: $40
- Total daily meal allowance: $80
- Alcohol is not reimbursable during solo meals; reasonable alcohol with clients is reimbursable

## Client Entertainment
- Pre-approval required for expenses over $200
- Maximum: $150 per person for client dinners
- Document: client name(s), company, business purpose
- Reasonable alcohol permitted with client meals

## Software and Subscriptions
- Software under $50/month: manager approval only
- Software $50–$500/month: manager + IT approval
- Software over $500/month: requires VP approval and IT security review
- All software must comply with IT security policy

## Professional Development
- See Benefits Guide for the $2,500 annual learning stipend
- Conference registration, travel, and accommodation count against this stipend
- Manager pre-approval required

## Home Office Equipment
- $500 one-time stipend for new hybrid/remote employees (see Remote Work Policy)
- Additional equipment requests require manager and IT approval

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses
- Personal entertainment
- Traffic or parking violations
- Clothing (unless required safety equipment)
- Pet care during travel
- Gym memberships during travel (use the wellness stipend instead)
- First-class air travel
- Spouse/partner travel expenses

## Expense Fraud
Falsifying expense reports is grounds for immediate termination and potential legal action. If you're unsure whether an expense is reimbursable, ask your manager or HR before incurring it.
